BIOGRAPHY

Laufey

Laufey Lín Jónsdóttir, known as Laufey, was born on April 23, 1999, in Reykjavík, Iceland. Coming from a musical family, she started playing piano and cello at a young age and debuted with the Iceland Symphony Orchestra at fifteen. She gained early recognition on Ísland Got Talent and The Voice Iceland before moving to the U.S. to study at Berklee College of Music. Combining jazz, classical, and modern influences, she rose to fame through social media in 2020, later releasing the acclaimed albums Everything I Know About Love (2022) and Bewitched (2023), the latter earning her a Grammy Award and international success.
